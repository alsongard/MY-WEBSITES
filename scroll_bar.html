CSS-only scroll driven animation are 🔥

Scroll-driven animation was somethng taht we used to rely on JavaScript for, with the help of scroll listeners and intersection observers.

Luckily for us, scroll-driven animations are coming to be a CS feature, and they make life so much simpler and more performant.
